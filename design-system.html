Elfin Market Design System — building consistency at a fast paced fintech startup

ABOUT ELFIN MARKET

Elfin Market is a P2P lending disruptor offering fair and flexible credit. Their unique selling point is total flexibility on borrowing, with iOS and Android apps serving as a core point of application process, withdrawals and repayments.

PLATFORMS

PROBLEM

Lack of Design System meant engineers had to build most of the front-end from scratch, delaying releases of new features. Inconsistencies between iOS and Android further added to the problem.

When I joined Elfin Market they already had a beta version of the app running, which was mostly designed by founders and help of external designer. With rapid growth in mind, the goal was to develop the app to have core functionality, which could not be possible without an established Design System of components.

Working closely with iOS and Android developers, 3 of us built a system that worked in existing flows and scaled to new features.

SOLUTIONS

Use of components

Together with iOS and Android engineers, we have established a base system of UI components, using the atomic principles and adapting them where necessary.

IMPACT

Establishing a solid foundation for Elfin Market's product was crucial for their plans of expansion. A few key wins:

  • 01 Consistency

    Visually defined colors, typography, spacing, icons and images established Elfin Market's brand and increased trust.

  • 02 Efficiency

    Using mostly prebuilt components meant we could ship features much faster.

  • 03 Code and design alignment

    Having one source of truth on Figma meant less miscommunication.
